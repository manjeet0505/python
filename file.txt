WHat is LLM ?
     An LLM (Large Language Model) is a type of artificial intelligence model that is trained on massive amounts of text data to understand, generate, and reason with human language.
     
     Examples of LLMs:

     GPT-4 / GPT-5 (by OpenAI)
     
     Gemini (by Google DeepMind)
     
     Claude (by Anthropic)
     
     LLaMA (by Meta)
     
     Mistral (open-source)
